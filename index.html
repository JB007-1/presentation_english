Presentation of Facts & Opinions of the project

Fact 1

The project used Scrumban methodology, combining Scrum's planning with Kanban's task visualization, allowing flexibility and iterative progress.

Opinion 1

The choice of the Scrumban methodology was ideal, as it allowed the team to adapt to changes and refine tasks efficiently.

Fact 2

CrediSystem is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed for "Ventas de Lizbeth," enabling offline functionality and mobile accessibility.

Opinion 2

CrediSystem represents a significant step towards modernizing small businesses, especially in improving their financial processes.

Fact 3

The application uses technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and Supabase, ensuring a robust architecture for financial management.

Opinion 3

The use of familiar technologies ensured faster development and higher quality output, demonstrating the team's expertise.

Fact 4

The main objective was to automate manual credit and payment management, reducing errors and improving efficiency.

Opinion 4

The decision to make the application a PWA is perfectly suited for the client's needs, as it ensures 24/7 accessibility without constant internet connectivity.

Fact 5

Development involved a clear division of roles, with tasks assigned based on each team member's expertise, such as database design, user interface creation, and backend programming.

Opinion 5

The team's collaboration and communication during development were exemplary, contributing significantly to the project's success.
